# MediaPrune Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 4, 2026

MediaPrune is a photo and video library cleanup app designed to help you review, organize, and delete media from your iPhone photo library.

This Privacy Policy explains how MediaPrune handles information when you use the app.

## Information We Access

MediaPrune may request access to your photo and video library so the app can show cleanup categories such as screenshots, large videos, duplicate photos, similar photos, and other photos.

Photo library access is used only to help you review and organize media on your device. MediaPrune does not upload your photos or videos to an external server.

## Photo and Video Processing

MediaPrune processes photos and videos locally on your device. The app may read limited metadata available through Apple's Photos framework, such as media type, creation date, dimensions, duration, and photo library identifiers, to organize items into review categories.

Your photos and videos are not sent to us, stored on our servers, or shared with third parties by MediaPrune.

## Limited Photos Access and Full Access

iOS allows you to choose Limited Photos Access or Full Access.

If you choose Limited Photos Access, MediaPrune can only review the photos and videos you selected in the iOS permission screen. Some cleanup categories may show fewer items because the app cannot see media outside your selected library access.

If you choose Full Access, MediaPrune can review your photo and video library locally on your device to show cleanup categories.

You can change photo library permissions at any time in the iOS Settings app.

## Deleting Photos and Videos

MediaPrune does not delete photos or videos automatically. Deletion only happens after you select items and confirm the delete action.

When you delete selected items through MediaPrune, the request is handled by Apple's Photos framework. Deleted photos and videos may be moved to the iOS Recently Deleted album, where they may remain for a limited time depending on your iOS settings.

## Data We Do Not Collect

MediaPrune does not require an account or login.

MediaPrune does not provide cloud sync.

MediaPrune does not use artificial intelligence features in version 1.0.0.

MediaPrune does not include paid subscriptions or in-app purchases in version 1.0.0.

MediaPrune does not include advertising at this time.

## Contact Support

If you contact support from the app, MediaPrune may open your email app with a support email draft. Sending that email is optional and controlled by you.

The support email may include app information such as app name, version, build number, platform, and operating system version so we can understand your support request. Do not include sensitive information in your message unless you choose to do so.

Contact: support@passmate.jp

## Children's Privacy

MediaPrune is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has contacted us or provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

## Security

MediaPrune is designed to process media locally on your device. No method of software operation or electronic communication is completely secure, but we aim to limit data handling by avoiding account systems, cloud uploads, and external photo or video processing in version 1.0.0.

## Future Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy if MediaPrune changes its features or data practices. For example, if advertising, paid features, analytics, or cloud services are added in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated to explain those changes.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by changing the effective date or publishing an updated version of this policy.
